About the club

Cricket that brings the community together

Falcon CC was founded in 2025 with a mission to grow cricket in Southern Connecticut. We compete in the SCL league and welcome players of all skill levels — from seasoned veterans to those just picking up a bat.

Competitive play

We compete in the SCL (Southern Connecticut League), with regular fixtures across the season.

Open to all

All backgrounds, ages, and skill levels are welcome. We believe cricket is better when it's inclusive.

Fresh start

Founded in 2025, we're building something special from the ground up in Connecticut.

Regular fixtures

Scheduled matches throughout the season so you always have a game to look forward to.
