Falcon CC — Est. 2025

Something different.
Something that lasts.

Falcon CC didn't start with a boardroom decision. It started with a group of people who wanted more — more intensity, more purpose, more of what makes cricket worth playing. Seasoned heads and hungry newcomers, all chasing the same thing.

No half measures. Every delivery, every innings, every match — full commitment.

01
A club worth belonging to
The culture here isn't an afterthought — it's the foundation. We hold each other to a standard, look after each other, and show up when it counts.
02
Taking cricket further
Southern Connecticut deserves more cricket. We're here to change that — opening the game up to people who've never had the chance to experience it.
03
Rooted in the region
We're not passing through. We're investing in this area — building a club that means something to the people and places around us, long after the season ends.
04
Unrelenting on the field
Enjoyment and intensity aren't opposites. We chase victories with everything we have — because that's the only way to truly respect the game and the people you share it with.
